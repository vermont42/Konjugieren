# Sound-Replacement Prompts for Claude in Chrome

These prompts are for use with the Claude in Chrome extension on [Pixabay Sound Effects](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/).

## Prompt 1: Gun-Sound Replacement (Single Shot)

I'm on Pixabay's sound-effects page. I need a very short, non-violent sound effect to replace a gun/laser-shot sound in a casual mobile game. The sound plays frequently (every time the player fires), so it must be brief (under 0.5 seconds) and unobtrusive — something that won't become annoying on repetition.

Important: the sound must NOT resemble a real gun, gunshot, or any weapon. Think along these lines — but use your own judgment and creativity:
- A short pop or "boop"
- A soft whoosh or puff of air
- A quick pluck or ping
- A tiny bubble pop
- A gentle zap or chirp

Please search broadly, consider a variety of sounds, and use your judgment to pick three good candidates. Download all three so I can audition them. Prioritize sounds that feel satisfying and game-like but are clearly non-violent.

## Prompt 2: Machine-Gun-Sound Replacement (Rapid Fire / Power-Up)

I'm on Pixabay's sound-effects page. I need a longer, more intense sound effect to replace a machine-gun/rapid-fire sound in a casual mobile game. This plays during a power-up moment, so it should feel exciting and energetic — but it must NOT sound anything like a gun, gunfire, or any weapon.

The sound should be roughly 1–3 seconds long and convey a sense of rapid, sustained energy. Think along these lines — but use your own judgment and creativity:
- A rapid drumroll or percussive burst
- A buzzing or whirring mechanical sound
- A rapid-fire series of pops, clicks, or chirps
- An accelerating whoosh or turbine spin-up
- A sizzling or crackling energy burst
- A rapid xylophone or marimba run

Please search broadly, consider a variety of sounds, and use your judgment to pick three good candidates. Download all three so I can audition them. Prioritize sounds that feel powerful and game-like but are unmistakably non-violent.
