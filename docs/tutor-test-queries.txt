Tutor Tool Test Queries
=======================
Each query is followed by what the tool should return and what the
model's response should contain. Reset the tutor session between tests.

--- Conjugationgroup coverage (German names) ---

1. How do you conjugate singen in the Präteritum?
   Tool returns: singen in the Past Indicative: ich sang, du sangst, er sang, wir sangen, ihr sangt, sie sangen
   Check: all six persons present, correct forms, says "Past Indicative" or "Präteritum"

2. What is the Perfekt of gehen?
   Tool returns: gehen in the Present Perfect Indicative: ich bin gegangen, du bist gegangen, ...
   Check: uses "sein" auxiliary (not "haben"), all six persons

3. Conjugate haben in the Präsens.
   Tool returns: haben in the Present Indicative: ich habe, du hast, er hat, wir haben, ihr habt, sie haben
   Check: irregular du/er forms (hast, hat)

4. What is the Konjunktiv II of sein?
   Tool returns: sein in the Past Conditional: ich wäre, du wärest, ...
   Check: uses wäre forms, not war forms

--- Conjugationgroup coverage (English names) ---

5. What is the past participle of trinken?
   Tool returns: getrunken (Past Participle)
   Check: single form, no persons listed, strong-verb participle

6. Conjugate laufen in the future tense.
   Tool returns: laufen in the Future Indicative: ich werde laufen, du wirst laufen, ...
   Check: werden + infinitive pattern, all six persons

7. How is sprechen conjugated in the present indicative?
   Tool returns: sprechen in the Present Indicative: ich spreche, du sprichst, er spricht, ...
   Check: e→i vowel change in du/er forms

--- Verb family variety ---

8. What is the Präteritum of studieren?
   Tool returns: studieren in the Past Indicative: ich studierte, du studiertest, ...
   Check: weak -ieren verb, regular -te endings

9. Conjugate wissen in the Präsens.
   Tool returns: wissen in the Present Indicative: ich weiß, du weißt, er weiß, wir wissen, ...
   Check: irregular modal-like verb, singular ≠ plural stem

10. What is the present participle of machen?
    Tool returns: machend (Present Participle)
    Check: single form, -end suffix

--- Edge cases and model behavior ---

11. What is the imperative of geben?
    Tool returns: geben in the Imperative: du gib, ihr gebt, wir geben wir, sie geben sie
    Check: model handles imperative persons (du/ihr/wir/sie, no ich/er)

12. How do you say "I would have sung" in German?
    Check: model should recognize this as Plusquamperfekt Konjunktiv II
    and call the tool for singen, returning: ich hätte gesungen

13. What is the difference between Präteritum and Perfekt?
    Check: model should answer from its own knowledge (grammar explanation),
    possibly calling the tool to illustrate with examples

14. How do you conjugate pizza?
    Check: tool returns an error (pizza is not a verb), model handles gracefully

15. Tell me about the weather.
    Check: model politely redirects to German verb topics
